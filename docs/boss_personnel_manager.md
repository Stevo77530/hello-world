# Boss Design Stub — The Personnel Manager

## Role
Level 1 end boss and embodiment of denied relief, staffing abuse, and bureaucratic violence.

## Appearance
- Massive dockside authority silhouette
- White hard hat (cracked/stained)
- Stained white button-up and sloppy tie
- Clipboard fused to arm/hand
- Chains, ratchet straps, radio parts, badges, heavy grime
- Hybrid look: management + dock labor + industrial corpse authority

## Intro Sequence (Locked)
1. Departure gate starts to open.
2. Intercom: “Your crew change request has been reviewed.”
3. Beat.
4. Intercom: “We’re short-handed, so fuck you.”
5. Gate slams shut.
6. The Personnel Manager enters arena.

## Attack List

### Phase 1 (Control + intimidation)
- **Clipboard Slam**: short-range impact cone
- **Ratchet Lash**: mid-range linear strike with stagger
- **Chain Sweep**: horizontal close-range area denial
- **Denial Burst**: radio-static pulse that disrupts visibility/audio clarity briefly

### Phase 2 (Aggressive enforcement)
Trigger: health threshold transition (placeholder target: 50%)
- Movement speed and combo cadence increase
- Voice line: **“No one leaves short-handed.”**
- Optional additions for implementation pass:
  - Hook pull into melee punish
  - Forward rush chain combo

## Phase Transition Presentation
- Static surge + intercom distortion
- Brief animation lock and posture shift
- Arena audio bed intensifies
- Reinforces escalation from paperwork authority to direct violence

## Death Sequence (Locked Line)
- Boss collapses and drops restraint gear
- Final line: **“Fine… cover your own shift…”**
- Exit gate unlock delay (short beat for emotional release)

## Thematic Purpose
The boss converts mundane labor-management cruelty into tangible combat threat. Beating The Personnel Manager is the player’s first proof that institutional denial can be confronted physically within this nightmare logic.
